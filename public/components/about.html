About

This is the About page for the AI4EA platform. This page would contain information about the project, its goals, and the team behind it.

Project Overview

The AI4EA platform is designed to visualize electricity load data across local regions (LGAs) in Africa. It provides tools for analyzing peak demand, load factors, and electrification rates across different countries and regions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible data visualization tools that help stakeholders make informed decisions about energy infrastructure and planning in Africa.
